About Me

My name is Trinity, and I am a musician located in Twin Falls, Idaho. I currently am in the process of becoming a front end developer. I have always had an intense curiosity for the unknown, and technology is the perfect field to satiate my appetite. Currently, I am enrolled in Promineo Tech's Coding Bootcamp as a front end developer. Soon after receiving my certificate of completion in May 2024, I will be doubling down to complete the back end program in June. Until this course, I have never touched a line of code in my life! If you want tag along with me on my journey to become an accomplished software developer, please connect with me! Up until 2024 I have been a musician, teaching guitar lessons to people of all ages. Music has always been an active interest in my life. Being both a student and a teacher has taught me that becoming great at anything takes extreme amounts of dedication. You have to be willing to fail; miserably. Many times. Until you stop failing. My ultimate belief is to always shoot for the moon. If you miss, at least you'll land among the stars. My unrelenting perseverance will continue to be my backbone for problem solving. Outside of my professional life, I enjoy many hobbies. My favorites are drawing, playing video games, hiking with my dogs, and reading. I love to learn, and educate myself on anything I haven't done before. I chose to become a software developer, because of the endless creativity. I love that my curiosity will be welcomed, and that I will never feel locked into one specific topic. I'm so excited to share my journey with you!
